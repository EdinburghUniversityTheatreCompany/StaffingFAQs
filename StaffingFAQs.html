Staffing FAQs

WHAT IS STAFFING?

Staffing is the obligation for all show teams to give back to the company. Members of the company will staff the building for your show, and in return, you have to staff a certain number of shifts.

HOW CAN I SIGN UP?

On the Members pages of the Bedlam website. If you can't get access, make sure you have bought membership for the current academic year and emailed Sophie on secretary@bedlamtheatre.co.uk to activate your website access.

WHAT'S THE DEADLINE?

You can staff at any point - before or after your show, but you must be signed up by the week after your show (eg. Week 6 for a Week 5 show). You will be informed of the specific date through your producer, who will also get a list of names the day before the deadline.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I MISS THE DEADLINE?

At midnight on the day of the deadline, you will receive an email informing you that you are in debt. This means that you can no longer become involved in Bedlam shows until your debt is cleared.

There is a 48 hour grace period where if you get in touch with me I can extend the deadline. This could be if you sign up to staff, if you have work commitments that mean your shifts don't get published until shortly beforehand, or any other good reason. The key is getting in touch early.

After the 48 hours has elapsed, your debt will remain until you actually staff the slots as required. The only exceptions are for welfare reasons, which if for any reason you don't want to discuss with me, can be discussed with Sophie Lee, Camilla Anvar Makhmudi, or Myles Westman, the company welfare contacts.

WHAT CAN I DO IF I HAVE ANY OTHER QUESTIONS?

You can message me (Jacob Matthew Holt) on Facebook or email foh@bedlamtheatre.co.uk.
